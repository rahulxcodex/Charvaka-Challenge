The Urban Futures Challenge 2025

A Charvaka initiative to design the next generation of sustainable cities, aligned with UN SDG 11.

Design a Tangible, Sustainable Reality

This challenge invites students, freelancers, and young architects to develop innovative solutions for real-world urban problems, guided by the principles of **SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities**.

Gain Experience

Tackle a realistic design challenge and build your portfolio.

Get Noticed

Showcase your vision to industry leaders and professionals.

Win Funding

Secure cash prizes to fuel your innovative ideas or career.

Make an Impact

Contribute to the global mission for sustainable urban lives.

Eligibility & Rules

Team Size

Teams must consist of 2 to 4 members.

Team Composition

All team members must be from the same college or institution. Cross-institutional teams are not permitted.

Age Limit

Participants must be between 16 and 28 years of age.

Participant Category

Open to all students (school/college), young architects, and freelancers.

Original Work

All submissions must be the original work of the team members.

One Entry

Each participant can only be a member of one team.

More Than a Competition: Our Internal Pledge

The Urban Futures Challenge is an extension of our core philosophy. At Charvaka, our commitment to SDG 11 begins at home. We empower our own team through continuous training in sustainable design, foster an internal culture of innovation, and actively work to reduce our own operational footprint. This challenge is a platform for external talent and a celebration of the values our team lives by every day.
