Introduction

Competition Organiser

The Charvaka Urban Futures Challenge is an international architecture competition that explores how affordability and design excellence can be harmonized to create innovative, high-quality housing solutions tailored to tomorrow's urban context.

Ultimately, this is not just a competition—it is a platform for shaping the future of cities. By bringing together a diverse global audience of creative professionals, it seeks to provoke new ideas, question outdated norms, and inspire the next generation of inclusive, high-quality urban development.

Competition is open to all. No professional qualification is required.

Eligibility & Rules

  • Team Size: 2 to 4 members.
  • Team Composition: All members must be from the same institution.
  • Age Limit: Participants must be between 16 and 28 years old.
  • Category: Open to all students, young architects, and freelancers.
  • Original Work: All submissions must be the original work of the team.

More Than a Competition: Our Pledge

The Urban Futures Challenge is an extension of our core philosophy. At Charvaka, our commitment to SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities begins at home. We empower our team through continuous training in sustainable design, foster a culture of innovation, and actively work to reduce our operational footprint. This challenge is a platform for external talent and a celebration of the values our team lives by every day. By participating, you join a global community dedicated to creating tangible, affordable, and sustainable realities for the cities of tomorrow.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I participate in the competition?

Click on "Register Now", fill in all the required fields, choose your payment method and submit your information. Once we receive your payment, we'll send you an email with your username and password to upload your submission.

How do I edit team member information?

You can add, remove, or edit team member information as often as you want in the upload panel, up until the submission deadline.

Can a student project win one of the main prizes?

Yes. If the jury panel selects a student submission for the top 3, it will automatically be awarded both the main prize and the student award.
